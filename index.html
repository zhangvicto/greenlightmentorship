Welcome to Green Light!
Green Light is a mentorship program based in Ontario, Canada, that pairs high school students with a Canadian university student mentor based on their interests, academic course needs and post-secondary program choice.

Our Mission

Green Light mentors aim to offer high school students support by providing advice, guidance, and extra help with their academics. Green Light’s mission is to help as many students as possible with their transition back to school or to university.

In addition to the mentorship program, Green Light provides access to free resources, virtual events and a supportive community on social media that connects students across the country.

FAQ
My mentor/mentee has not reached out to me, even though I emailed them. What should I do?
The first step we suggest is to simply email them again! There is a high chance that they have not checked their email yet, or that the email got lost somewhere in their mailbox. If they still do not reply after a while, we encourage you to fill out our re-match form, and we will find you another mentor ASAP. How should I approach my mentee/mentor? How do I initiate a conversation?
The best way to do this is to simply introduce yourself! Introduce your name, your school and program or program of interest, and anything you’d be willing to help with or need help with! Whether this is answering questions about the school, the program, or with course help, make sure to let them know. Just simply ask your match about the kind of help they are offering/looking for! Mentees, remember that your mentors are here to offer their support and guidance, so do not be afraid of asking questions!

Some topics / prompts to ask your mentee/mentor:
Favourite quarantine activity
Favourite part of university
Extracurricular / Academic Interests
Other programs that you applied to
As a mentor / mentee, can I be paired up with more than one person? If I do, what should I do?
Yes! Since this has been brought to our attention, we have made some new changes to our form. For new mentors and mentees signing up, you now have the option of choosing how many mentors / mentees you would like to be paired up with. If you have already been matched and would like to be matched again with an additional mentor / mentee, please email us and let us know how many mentors or mentees you would like to be paired with. Another avenue to be re-matched or matched with additional mentees will be a form that will be posted in early May, so keep a look out for that. We do our best to accommodate everyone, with the diverse amount of interest we were provided.
When are matches sent out? Is there a signup deadline?
We try our very best to send out a new round of matches every Wednesday night so that we can revisit previous un-matched mentors or mentees as well as match our new sign ups. There is no deadline to sign up, we will have the form open for everyone for as long as there is interest.
What should I do if my mentor/mentee is not in the program or field of interest I stated in my form?
If this is the case, we as a team truly apologize for the mistake in the match. We try our very best to match to the best of our abilities. However, oftentimes program names can be unique at every university. Please email us, as we would gladly re-match both mentors and mentees, so that you can receive the best personalized guidance and support. We also will have a re-match form coming out in early May, if you feel more comfortable just filling out a form.
